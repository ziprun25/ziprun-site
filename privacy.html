Privacy Policy

Effective date:

ZipRun (“the App”) is a simple arcade game. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to the minimum required to operate ads and leaderboards on Apple devices.

What we collect

  • Advertising data (via Google Mobile Ads SDK). The App uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, coarse location, and performance metrics to serve and measure ads. We do not receive or store this data directly.
  • Game Center data (Apple). If you enable Game Center, Apple handles authentication and leaderboard data. We do not receive Game Center account information.
  • Crash and diagnostics (Apple). Optional analytics/crash logs may be shared with us through Apple’s developer tools in aggregate form.

What we do not collect

  • No account registration.
  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or precise location.
  • No direct tracking across other apps or websites by us.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK – Privacy • SDK disclosure
  • Apple Game Center – Apple Privacy

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided information to us, contact ziprun25@outlook.com and we will address it promptly.

Data retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Advertising data is processed by Google according to their policies. Game Center data is stored by Apple.
